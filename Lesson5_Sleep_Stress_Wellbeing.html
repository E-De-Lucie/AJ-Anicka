📖 Key Vocabulary — Health & Well-being
SECTION 2
well-being
the state of being healthy and happy
"Sleep is essential for your well-being."
exhausted
extremely tired, completely without energy
"I was so exhausted I fell asleep on the bus."
to concentrate/focus
to concentrate/focus your attention on something
"I can't concentrate/focus when I'm tired."
stressed out
feeling anxious and overwhelmed by pressure
"She's really stressed out about her exams."
to affect
to have an impact on something or someone
"Lack of sleep affects your mood."
a habit
something you do regularly, often without thinking
"I have a habit of checking my phone at night."

📐 Grammar: Modal Verbs for Advice & Obligation
SECTION 4
We use modal verbs to say what is necessary, recommended, or not needed. The form is always: modal + infinitive (without "to").
Modal Meaning Example
should advice / recommendation (it's a good idea) You should go to bed earlier.
shouldn't advice against (it's a bad idea) You shouldn't use your phone before sleep.
have to obligation from outside (a rule or necessity) I have to wake up at 6 for school.
don't have to no obligation (it's not necessary — but OK if you want) You don't have to sleep 9 hours, but it helps.
must strong obligation — from yourself or a rule I must stop scrolling at midnight.
Task: Complete the sentences — choose the best modal verb.
1. You drink coffee in the evening — it makes it harder to sleep.
2. You get 8–10 hours of sleep every night if you're a teenager. It's really important.
3. I get up at 5:30 — school starts at 7 and I live far away.
4. You exercise every day, but even a walk helps.
5. She sleep more — she looks exhausted all the time.
6. Students use their phones during lessons — it's against the rules.
🗣 Speaking — Your Experience
SECTION 5
Your Routine
What time do you go to bed on school days? Is it different at weekends? What do you usually do in the last hour before sleep?
Stress & School
When do you feel most stressed? What do you do to relax? Does stress affect your sleep?
Give Advice!
Your friend sleeps only 5 hours, drinks 3 coffees a day and scrolls TikTok until 2am. What should / shouldn't they do? Give at least 4 pieces of advice using modal verbs.
Big Question
Is it possible to be healthy as a teenager in today's world — with school pressure, phones, and social media? Or is it a losing battle?
Useful phrases — try to use them!
"I think you should…" "You really shouldn't…" "In my experience…" "I have to admit that…" "On one hand… on the other hand…" "I'm not sure, but…" "That depends on…"
✏️ Quick Writing Task
SECTION 6

Write 4–6 sentences about your own habits and health. Use at least 3 different modal verbs (should / have to / must / don't have to / shouldn't).

Start with: "To be healthier, I think I should…" — and then be honest! 😊
